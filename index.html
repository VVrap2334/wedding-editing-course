Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to help you make the right decision for your career transformation

Do I need prior video editing experience to join?

Absolutely not! Our workshop is specifically designed for complete beginners to intermediate level editors. We start from the very basics and gradually move to advanced techniques with hands-on practice. 60% of our students are complete beginners who become confident professional editors by the end.

What software will we be using in the workshop?

We'll primarily use Adobe Premiere Pro and DaVinci Resolve (both have free versions available). Don't worry if you don't have them installed - we'll guide you through the complete setup process and provide installation support before the workshop starts. Both software work on Windows and Mac.

Will I get access to workshop recordings if I miss a session?

Yes! All registered participants get lifetime access to HD workshop recordings, practice files, templates, and bonus materials. You can revisit any concept anytime and learn at your own pace even after the live sessions. However, we highly recommend attending live for real-time Q&A and interaction.

Is there any certification provided upon completion?

Absolutely! You'll receive a verified Certificate of Completion from Video Visionary, which you can add to your portfolio, LinkedIn profile, and resume. This certificate is recognized in the industry and adds significant credibility to your editing skills when approaching clients.

What computer specifications do I need for the workshop?

Minimum requirements: 8GB RAM, Intel i5 or AMD equivalent processor, and 50GB free storage. The software works on both Windows (10+) and Mac (macOS 10.14+). We'll provide detailed system requirements and optimization tips after registration to ensure smooth performance during the workshop.

Is there ongoing support after the workshop ends?

Yes! You'll get lifetime access to our exclusive WhatsApp community of 500+ professional editors where you can ask questions, share work, get feedback, find job opportunities, and network with peers. Rajesh sir is actively involved and provides ongoing guidance and career tips.

What if I'm not satisfied with the workshop?

We offer a 100% money-back guarantee if you're not completely satisfied within the first day of the workshop. However, 99% of our students are thrilled with the results and continue to benefit from the community and materials long after the workshop ends. Your success is our top priority.

How quickly can I start earning after the workshop?

Most of our students start earning within 30 days of completing the workshop. We provide business guidance, portfolio building tips, and client acquisition strategies. Many students have covered their course fee within their first paid project. The key is to implement what you learn immediately.
