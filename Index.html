About Rajesh S & Video Visionary

Learn from a professional with over 13 years of industry experience

Passionate Wedding Filmmaker & Educator

Rajesh S is a passionate and highly experienced wedding filmmaker with over 13 years in the wedding industry. With a deep love for storytelling through visuals, he has evolved into a professional video editor, colorist, and instructor.

Over the past 1.5 years, Rajesh has transitioned into teaching and mentoring aspiring editors and creatives. His mission is to empower individuals in the wedding visual arts industry with professional-level editing, color grading, photography, album design, and video creation skills.
